Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do I place an order?
Browse our collection, select your desired phone, choose specifications (color, storage), and click "Add to Cart". Once you're ready, proceed to checkout, fill in your shipping details, and complete the payment. You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), cash on delivery (COD), and mobile wallets. All online payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Yes, you can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact our customer service team immediately if you need to make changes.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 2-5 business days within Cairo and Giza, and 3-7 business days for other governorates. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.
Is shipping free?
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over 1000 EGP across Egypt. For orders under 1000 EGP, a flat shipping fee of 50 EGP applies.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order on our Track Order page or directly through the courier's website. You'll also receive updates at each shipping milestone.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 14-day return policy from the date of delivery. The phone must be in its original condition with all accessories, packaging, and documentation. Returns are free for defective items; a restocking fee may apply for change-of-mind returns.
Do your phones come with a warranty?
Yes! All phones come with an official manufacturer's warranty ranging from 1 to 2 years depending on the brand and model. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures. Extended warranty options are also available at checkout.
How do I initiate a return?
Visit our Returns page, enter your order number and email, and follow the instructions. You'll receive a return shipping label via email. Pack the item securely and drop it off at the designated courier location. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the return.
Are your phones authentic?
Absolutely! We only sell 100% authentic, brand-new phones sourced directly from authorized distributors. Every device comes with original packaging, accessories, and official warranty documentation. We guarantee authenticity.
Do you sell used or refurbished phones?
No, we exclusively sell brand-new, factory-sealed phones. We do not offer used or refurbished devices. Every phone you purchase from us is in pristine, unopened condition.
Can I request a phone that's not listed?
Yes! Contact our customer service team with the model and specifications you're looking for. We'll do our best to source it for you and provide a quote within 24-48 hours.
Do I need an account to place an order?
No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save addresses, view order history, and receive exclusive deals and early access to sales.
How can I contact customer support?
Our customer support team is available 24/7 via phone (+20 123 456 7890), email (info@mobilestore.com), or live chat on our website. We typically respond within 1 hour during business hours and within 3 hours outside business hours.
