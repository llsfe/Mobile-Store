Return Policy

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • Returned within 14 days of delivery
  • In its original condition with all accessories, packaging, and documentation
  • Unused and undamaged (except for defective items)
  • IMEI/Serial number matches the original purchase
Free Returns for Defective Items If your item is defective or damaged upon arrival, we'll cover all return shipping costs. Just contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items returned after 14 days from delivery
  • Items with broken seals or activated warranties (unless defective)
  • Items with physical damage caused by misuse
  • Items missing original packaging or accessories

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will:

  • Send you an email notification confirming receipt
  • Inspect the item within 2-3 business days
  • Process your refund to the original payment method
  • Refunds typically appear in your account within 5-7 business days
Restocking Fee A 10% restocking fee may apply for change-of-mind returns on opened items. Defective items and wrong shipments are exempt from this fee.

Exchanges

We currently don't offer direct exchanges. If you need a different item, please return the original product for a refund and place a new order for the item you want.
