Our Story

Building Egypt's most trusted mobile phone marketplace

Mobile Store was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make premium smartphones accessible to everyone in Egypt. What started as a small shop in Cairo has grown into one of the country's most trusted online mobile phone retailers.

We understand that buying a smartphone is a significant investment. That's why we're committed to providing not just the best products, but also the best customer experience. Every phone we sell comes with an official warranty, and our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist you.

Today, we serve thousands of happy customers across Egypt, from Cairo to Alexandria, Giza to Aswan. Our success is built on trust, quality, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.
