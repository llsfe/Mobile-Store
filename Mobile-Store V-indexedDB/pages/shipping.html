Common Shipping Questions

What if I'm not home during delivery?
Our courier will attempt delivery up to 3 times. If unsuccessful, you can arrange a pickup from the nearest courier office or reschedule delivery.
Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
Yes, you can change your delivery address within 2 hours of placing your order. Contact our customer service team immediately to make changes.
Do you ship to P.O. boxes?
No, we require a physical address for delivery as signature confirmation is required for all orders.
What happens if my package is damaged?
If your package arrives damaged, don't accept it. Contact our customer service immediately and we'll arrange a replacement at no extra cost.
Are there any additional customs fees?
No, all our products are sourced locally in Egypt. There are no customs fees or import duties.
